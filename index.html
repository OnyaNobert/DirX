Instructions

Welcome to DirX! Here are the instructions:

  1. Each player chooses a direction for their pieces: + or x.
  2. Click on a piece to select it.
  3. Click on an empty square to move the selected piece.
  4. Moves are either diagonal (x) or linear (+).
  5. Diagonal moves cover two squares, and linear moves cover one square.
  6. Capture opponent's pieces by moving to their square.
  7. Enjoy the game!
